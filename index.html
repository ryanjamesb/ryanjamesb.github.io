Welcome to Bowker's Fiberglass

Welcome to Bowker’s Fiberglass, a family owned and operated business with more than 50 years experience in the industry. We are located in Zeeland, Michigan just 15 minutes West of Grand Rapids. We've been servicing West Michigan for over 20 years. Our shop is big enough to facilitate most trailerable boats up to 50′ in length. We specialize in boats and personal watercraft, but if it’s fiberglass we can fix it.

A picture is worth a thousand words, and you can see lots of them on our site, but what doesn’t show through is how committed we are to each of our customers.

We know that in the long run taking care of each individual is what establishes a family business. Our business was built on word of mouth from our many satisfied customers. Come experience the personal attention only a smaller business can provide.
