Who We Are

Bowker's Fiberglass is a family owned and operated business with more than 50 years experience in the industry. We are located in Zeeland, Michigan just 15 minutes West of Grand Rapids. We have been servicing West Michigan for over 15 years. The shop is big enough to facilitate most trailerable boats up to 50' in length. We offer mobile service as well. We specialize in boats and personal watercraft, but if it's fiberglass we can fix it.
